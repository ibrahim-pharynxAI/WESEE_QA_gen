## 237. Credit Notes

- (i) Credit Notes IAFT-1711 is only to be used for the despatch of stores which are the property of the Ministry of Defence at the time of despatch and on which freight charges are debitable to Defence Services Estimates. Credit Notes must be tendered to the   railway authorities with the consignment. Such consignments cannot be sent 'freight to pay' .
- (ii) In the case of wharfage or demurrage charges payable to railways; a separate credit note will be issued.
